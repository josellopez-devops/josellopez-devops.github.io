About

The person who owns the hard endpoint problem

Across thirteen years I have built, secured, and scaled enterprise endpoint platforms. Today I lead endpoint platform strategy and engineering for a global fleet of over 150,000 devices, with ownership across Microsoft Intune, Jamf Pro, Entra ID conditional access, device compliance, host hardening, vulnerability remediation, automation, and major incident response.

I lead a team of eleven engineers across Apple and enterprise mobility services, setting technical direction while staying hands on, close enough to write the policy, harden the host, and lead the room when something breaks at scale. I bring people along rather than dictating to them, and I would rather grow an engineer than escalate past one.

I am known for turning complex endpoint, identity, and Zero Trust requirements into practical platform standards that reduce risk, improve compliance visibility, and keep the user experience clean.

Security, Risk, Infrastructure, and business teams trust me to own difficult endpoint problems from strategy through execution. I size controls to real risk, prove outcomes rather than effort, and measure what I ship.
